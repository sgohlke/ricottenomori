Ricotte no Mori: The story

As I open my eyes I look up to big trees. 'Where am I? What has happend?' I thought. I look up into the sky, but there are only big trees above me. But what is this yellow, glittering thing on the ground. As I take a close look I notice it is a slime.



It is a JellySlime that seems to be interested in me. As I try to move left it seems to follow me. But what is this rustle? Suddenly two blue creatures jump out of the darkness. What will happen now? Will JellySlime and I be able to beat them? Will we find our way out of this forrest?
